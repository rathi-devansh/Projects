## 📌 Overview

The Vyapar Partner App is built to strengthen the partner ecosystem by making it easier for partners to:

- Submit and track leads
- Monitor earnings and performance
- Participate in incentive programs
- Access sales and marketing tools

The product focuses heavily on reducing friction, improving usability, and increasing engagement frequency, making the mobile app more effective than the web dashboard.


## 🎯 Business Objective


- Increase partner-driven revenue
- Improve referral → conversion rates
- Boost partner engagement frequency
- Strengthen retention and satisfaction


#### The app redesign prioritizes:

- Faster workflows
- Better accessibility
- Intuitive UI
- Higher daily usage


## 🧠 Product Thinking


This app is designed as a partner growth engine, not just a dashboard:

- Problem	Solution
- Low engagement	Feed, contests, notifications
- Friction in lead submission	QR + contacts + manual upload
- Lack of visibility	Real-time dashboards & reports
- Low motivation	Tier system + incentives
- Weak enablement	Learning hub + marketing materials
